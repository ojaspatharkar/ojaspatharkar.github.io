ADI: Publishing and Sharing Your Site
Important:
This article refers to Wix ADI. Click here if you are looking for information regarding the Editor. 
Publish your site to make it live on the internet. You can edit or change your site at any time, even after it's published! 

To publish your site:

  • Click Publish on the top bar inside ADI.

To share your site:

  1. Publish your site. 
  2. Click the drop-down menu under Site. 
  3. Click View Published Site. 
  4. Copy your URL from the Site Address field. 
  5. Send the URL to friends and family. 
Note:
If you haven't connected a domain to your site, the URL will appear as follows:  username.wixsite.com/sitename/page-url. 
Tip:
You can publish your site as many times as you want. Only the most recent version will be live.
